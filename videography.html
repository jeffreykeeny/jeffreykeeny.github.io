VIDEOGRAPHY

This page is a compilation of film work I have done in the past. The majority of the films were done as personal projects and created using Final Cut Pro and/or Adobe Premiere. I am currently in the process of developing more videography work and developing an understanding of Adobe After Effects. My pursuit in film is still in progress...stay tuned.

Love At First Site

Created in Spring of 2015

This short film was created for UC Berkeley's Design Innovation: Visual Communication for Designers. The task was to create an informative and visual short film that educates the public on a particular topic, in this case online dating. With a team of four designers, we created this short film in one day to highlight three main benefits and advantages of pursuing a digital form of dating in the modern day.

Royals

Created in Summer of 2014

This is a music video of a personal friend, Gio Thomas. The song is a cover of Lorde's Royals, sung by Gio herself and composed by her musical team. This was an amateur mus video filmed and edited by myself using the Sony Nex Alpha and Final Cut Pro, respectively.

Walnut High School Dance

Created in Summer of 2013

This is a film montage of Walnut High School's Dance Team and Walnut High All Male (WHAM). This was a personal project that I completed to highlight the performances of both dance teams during the 2012 to 2013 school year.

In 2012, WHS's Dance Team won 1st Place at the USA Dance National competition. They took 2nd Place in the Open Large Dance, 3rd Place in Open Prop, and 5th Place in Championship Kick.
