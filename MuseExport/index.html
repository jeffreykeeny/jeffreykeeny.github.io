Hello, my name is Jeff.

I am a third-year student at the University of California Berkeley, studying Business Administration, focusing on marketing consultancy and brand management, with a minor in Design Innovation.

 

I am a designer who is avidly interested in human centered design. Professionally, I work as a instructor & facilitator for UC Berkeley's Introduction to Photoshop & Illustrator DeCal, a semester-long course that teaches students the fundamentals of the Adobe softwares and the principles of graphic & visual design. Additionally, I am a production designer for ImagiCal, UC Berkeley's fully-student run advertising agency and creative consultancy organization.

 

Recently, I've become intrigued by the intricacies of the human experience, and how it is an essential ingredient for the design of products, digital applications, and technology. Furthermore, I'm fascinated by the process through which these products captivate consumers through the use of strategic marketing and effective brand management. I hope to pursue a career in product and user interface/experience design and marketing/brand consultancy.

 

If you are interested in getting to know me better and for business inquiries, please do not hesitate to contact me through e-mail or any other form of social media. I love meeting new people - let's get in touch.
