JEFFREY KEENY CHEN

Hello from San Francisco. Originally from the suburbs of Los Angeles County, I am currently a third-year student at the University of California Berkeley, studying Business Administration, focusing on marketing, with a minor in Design Innovation.

 

I am a graphic designer who is avidly interested in human centered design. I currently work as a student instructor for UC Berkeley's Introduction to Photoshop & Illustrator DeCal, a semester-long course that educates students on the basic fundamentals of Adobe softwares and graphic design. Recently, I've become intrigued by the intricacies of the human mind, and how that comes into play when designing web and mobile applications. I hope to pursue a career in the creative industry, working with people who see the world in CMYK.

 

My interests also include good music (emphasis on the good), the sport of golf, the way the universe works astronomically, going on spontaneous adventures, and anything that involves me consuming delicious food.

One day in the near future, I'd like to travel to all seven continents, absorbing in the unique cultures from around the world.

 

If life has ever given me a lemon, it must have tasted of charisma. I am a unique people person - an extroverted introvert, some would call it. I find solace in being around good company and good vibes, though my independence sometimes precedes me. At the end of the day, I'm just your average human and professional daydreamer.

 

If you are interested in getting to know me better or you have any questions/concerns or for any business inquiries, please do not hesitate to shoot me an e-mail or reach me through any form of social media. I love meeting new people. Let's get in touch.
